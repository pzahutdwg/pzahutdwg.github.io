Guide

  • While cooked fish does a lot for you, fish soup does more!
    • You can find the pot on the counter.
    • You can find water at the well, or in the shop.
    • You can cool food on the stove.
    • If you don't feel like cooking, you can buy the food from the shop.
  • Butterdog will aid you in battle! He can be found in the forest.
    • Butterdog can also sniff around in the forest to find loot. If you see smoke coming from an object in the forest, click on it and butterdog will uncover it!
  • You can jump over the holes using magic feathers.
    • Magic feathers are found when Butterdog eats the Magic Chicken.
  • The dungeon boss drops a power bracelet. It can be used to move heavy things.
    • The dungeon is filled with nasty guards, come prepared!
    • The dungeon key is found in the forest buried under some leaves. Butterdog can dig it up, but you will need to look for it.
  • Don't have money? Sell things! Don't have things to sell? Rob the store!
    • Robbing the store can be done by taking food from behing the counter.
    • If the cashier sees you stealing food, she will alert the guards.
    • There is a gangster hiding in the trees. He will remove your bounty, but for a price.

Pro Tips:

  • Before going into the dungeon, find Butterdog and stock up on fish soup.
  • Dont forget to buy a torch from the store. The dungeon is dark!
  • Try to avoid the dungeon guards, they are tough!
  • Try to level up before fighting the boss. You're gonna need it!
