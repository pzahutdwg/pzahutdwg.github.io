Get to School 5 is a game where you need to get to school. Many things might stop you along the way though. Maybe you will get trapped in a dungeon, or maybe you will get lost in a forest! Before you go to school, there are many fun things to do around town. You could go fishing, cook food, or maybe even rob a store!

Maybe you will get to school eventually. Why don't you play and find out? If you are stuck, check out the game guide to help you out. This game was made using RPG maker MZ. I made it for a school project.
